Know Your Astrologer

He first analyses the day astrologically and explains the planetary conditions prevailing on that particular day. His impeccable analytical skills and in-depth knowledge in astrological science makes this part an invaluable one as the osition and placement of planets and stars on any given day have their own effect on any individual based on his own astrological signs. He then proceeds to deliver the prediction for each and every zodiac (Rashi) which are widely accepted as perfect reflections of the zodiac conditions.
